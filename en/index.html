About

A gaze.
A voice.
A commitment.

Kany J. Henry is a Congolese writer-director, screenwriter, editor and series creator based in Conakry. Through KWG Films, he develops stories that explore the shadows of our societies while revealing their strength, beauty and resilience.

“Directing begins on the page: rhythm, atmosphere and silence are integral to the writing process.”
